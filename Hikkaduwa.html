Hikkaduwa

General info

Hikkaduwa has a great long beach and lively town, so there's plenty of other stuff to do in addition to surfing.

The surf

Hikkaduwa's long beach has at least two spots. One is further out, with bigger swell and for more advanced shredders. The other one is close to the shore, with fun left and rights for days. Beware of the left-handers, remember to bail early to avoid really shallow waters. Respect the locals and be friendly, you'll make some buddies in the water in no time.

Where to stay?

Great views, great pool, amazing breakfast. What else could you ask for? Do yourself a favor and stay at Hotel Finlanka.
